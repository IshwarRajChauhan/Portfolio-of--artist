About his Exhibitions

Amit Bhar is a well-known Indian artist recognized for his exceptional portrayal of Indian culture, landscapes, and heritage. He often draws inspiration from rural Bengal and the old-world charm of cities like Kolkata and Varanasi. His art is noted for blending realism with an atmospheric play of light and shade, giving a unique vibrancy and soul to his works.
Solo and Group Exhibitions:
Amit Bhar has showcased his works extensively in India and internationally. His exhibitions typically highlight: Indian spirituality, urban nostalgia, rural landscapes, and everyday life.
His signature style includes oil paintings with a focus on old architectures, temples, ghats, and nature
Prominent Art Exhibitions:
Jehangir Art Gallery in Mumbai, which frequently hosts his shows. Academy of Fine Arts in Kolkata. India Habitat Centre, New Delhi
International Recognition:
Amit Bhar's works have also been exhibited globally, attracting appreciation for their attention to detail, serene tones, and emotional depth. His exhibitions have spanned countries such as:The United States, The United Kingdom, Singapore, Canada
Art Fairs:
Amit Bhar regularly participates in leading Indian art fairs like: India Art Festival, Kalaghoda Art Festival
His series featuring the ghats of Varanasi and serene temples of rural Bengal are particularly celebrated.
His works depict a spiritual connect that resonates with Indian heritage lovers, making his exhibitions popular among collectors and art connoisseurs.
The blend of modern techniques with traditional imagery makes his exhibitions a unique experience.
